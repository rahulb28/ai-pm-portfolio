About this portfolio
These prototypes were built to demonstrate applied AI product thinking — not just theory. Each one maps directly to real product work: ACMI Lite reflects the multi-agent system I prototyped during my AI PM certification; the Auction Simulator reflects 6+ years working with bidding dynamics across Google, Trivago, Tripadvisor and Kayak; the A/B Analyzer demonstrates core statistical concepts behind experiment design — including significance testing, confidence intervals, and sample size estimation.

The PRD Generator uses the Claude API to generate structured PRDs from product ideas — demonstrating how AI tools can accelerate PM workflows. The Bid Health Monitor mirrors the platform health metrics framework I established for a $1B annual ad spend operation.
